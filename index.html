Our Business Scope

Hydrology & Climate Risk Analysis

Rainfall-runoff analysis, watershed modelling, flood frequency estimation, and climate impact assessment to support water resources planning and infrastructure evaluation.

Hydraulic Modelling & Flood Risk Analysis

Hydraulic and flow modelling for rivers, dams, spillways, drainage systems, and engineering structures to assess flood risk, flow capacity, scouring, sediment movement, and infrastructure performance.

Coastal & Marine Modelling

Coastal hydrodynamic modelling for tides, waves, tsunami, sediment movement, and coastal inundation to support coastal planning and marine hazard assessment.

Hydropower Support

Technical modelling and assessment support for hydropower infrastructure, including water availability, river flow conditions, intake performance, sedimentation risk, flood hazard, and environmental flow.

Wind Energy Support

Wind energy support through wind-flow analysis, turbine site assessment, turbulence and wake evaluation, and environmental condition review for wind turbine and wind farm planning.
