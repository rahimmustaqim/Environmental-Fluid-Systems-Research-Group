Hydrology · Climate

Hydrology and Climate Analysis

Analysis of rainfall, runoff, watershed response, flood frequency, and climate-related water risks for planning and infrastructure evaluation.

Rainfall-runoff analysis Watershed modelling Flood frequency estimation Climate impact scenarios
Hydraulic · Flood

Flood and River Engineering

River hydraulic modelling to evaluate flood extent, water level, velocity, overflow risk, river capacity, and mitigation alternatives.

Flood inundation mapping River capacity assessment HEC-RAS 1D/2D modelling Mitigation scenario comparison
Coastal · Marine

Coastal and Marine Modelling

Numerical modelling for waves, tides, currents, tsunami-related processes, sediment transport, shoreline response, and coastal hazard assessment.

Hydrodynamic modelling Wave and current analysis Sediment transport modelling Coastal hazard assessment
Groundwater · Subsurface

Groundwater and Subsurface Flow

Groundwater flow and contaminant transport modelling for aquifer assessment, pumping impact, recharge systems, seepage, and water security studies.

MODFLOW-based modelling Groundwater flow analysis Contaminant transport Recharge and well assessment
CFD · Fluid Dynamics

CFD and Environmental Fluid Dynamics

CFD-based analysis for complex flow behaviour, turbulence, dispersion, mixing, recirculation, and interaction between flow and engineering structures.

Flow and turbulence simulation Dispersion and mixing analysis Fluid-structure interaction Design performance testing
