About Environmental Fluid Systems Research Group

Environmental Fluid Systems Research Group is a research-based company focusing on environmental fluid dynamics, hydrological analysis, coastal engineering, climate-related studies, and computational modelling. We support academic, governmental, and industrial projects through data-driven analysis, numerical modelling, and practical engineering insight.

Company Information

Who We Are

We combine engineering knowledge, numerical simulation, field data interpretation, and scientific communication to provide reliable support for water, coastal, and environmental projects.

Our Vision

To become a trusted research and consulting partner in environmental fluid systems, supporting sustainable infrastructure, coastal resilience, and water-related decision making.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality modelling, analysis, and scientific interpretation for projects related to hydrodynamics, hydrology, flooding, sediment transport, climate impacts, and environmental engineering.

What We Do

Core Business Scope

Our work focuses on applying scientific and engineering methods to understand, simulate, and solve environmental fluid-related problems.

Coastal & Hydrodynamic Modelling

Simulation and analysis of coastal flows, waves, currents, tsunami-related processes, inundation, and hydrodynamic interactions with coastal structures or floating objects.

Hydrology & Flood Analysis

Rainfall-runoff analysis, flood risk assessment, watershed evaluation, river flow modelling, and water-related hazard interpretation for planning and engineering studies.

CFD & Environmental Fluid Dynamics

Computational Fluid Dynamics analysis for water, air, sediment, and environmental transport processes using numerical simulation and visualization.

Climate & Environmental Analysis

Climate data interpretation, environmental impact analysis, rainfall trend evaluation, and support for adaptation-related studies.

Data Processing & Visualization

Processing of scientific and engineering datasets into clear graphs, maps, reports, and visual outputs for technical communication and decision support.

Technical Reports & Research Support

Preparation of technical reports, research manuscripts, project documentation, scientific figures, and publication-ready materials.
