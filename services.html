Why Coastal Modelling Matters

Better understanding today. Better decisions for our coasts tomorrow.

Waves Wave climate, nearshore transformation, and design conditions.

Currents Coastal circulation, navigation condition, and transport pathways.

Why Groundwater Modelling Matters

Invisible water systems need clear numerical interpretation.

The Purpose

Groundwater models help understand aquifer behaviour, pumping impact, recharge processes, seepage, and contaminant migration before they affect water security.

Aquifer System Subsurface layers, groundwater table, and flow direction.
Pumping Wells Drawdown, production impact, and sustainable abstraction.
Recharge Rainfall infiltration, injection wells, and managed recharge.
Contaminants Pollutant transport, plume migration, and concentration changes.

Why CFD & Environmental Fluid Dynamics Matters

Complex flow behaviour can be tested virtually before physical implementation.

The Purpose

CFD helps analyse turbulence, recirculation, dispersion, mixing, and fluid-structure interaction in hydraulic, coastal, and environmental engineering systems.

Models help us understand
Turbulence Flow separation, vortices, eddies, and recirculation zones.
Structures Flow interaction around piers, gates, channels, and barriers.
Dispersion Mixing, pollutant transport, scalar movement, and spreading.
Numerics Mesh, boundary conditions, validation, and model interpretation.
