Our Business Scope

Flood Modelling and River Capacity Analysis

River flood assessment using rainfall-runoff and hydraulic modelling to evaluate inundation extent, overflow risk, and river capacity improvement scenarios.

View Project

Groundwater Flow and Contaminant Transport

Groundwater modelling using MODFLOW-based tools to simulate flow systems, recharge, production wells, injection wells, and contaminant migration processes.

View Project

Coastal and Marine Modelling

Coastal hydrodynamic modelling for tides, waves, tsunami, sediment movement, and coastal inundation to support coastal planning and marine hazard assessment.

View Project

Hydrology and Climate Analysis

Rainfall-runoff analysis, watershed modelling, flood frequency estimation, and climate impact assessment to support water resources planning and infrastructure evaluation.

View Project

CFD and Environmental Fluid Dynamics

Computational fluid dynamics simulation for complex flow behaviour, turbulence, dispersion, and interaction between fluid motion and engineering structures.

View Project
