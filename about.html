About Environmental Fluid Systems Research Group

Numerical Modelling, Data Analysis, and Technical Support for Water, Coastal, Hydropower, and Wind Energy Systems

Environmental Fluid Systems Research Group supports engineering consultants, researchers, and public institutions through science-based analysis in hydrology, hydraulic modelling, coastal systems, hydropower, wind energy, and environmental infrastructure.

Our Approach

```

Research-Based

We combine engineering judgement, scientific literature, numerical modelling, and data-driven analysis to support technical decision-making.

Water and Flow Systems

Our work focuses on hydrology, hydraulics, coastal processes, sediment movement, hydropower infrastructure, and wind-flow-related technical support.

Collaborative Support

We support consultants, institutions, researchers, and project teams through modelling assistance, technical review, training, and scientific reporting.
