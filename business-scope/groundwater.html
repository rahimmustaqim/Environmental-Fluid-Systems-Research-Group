Why this matters

Groundwater Flow and Contaminant Transport

Groundwater modelling is important for understanding how water moves below the ground surface and how human activities may affect aquifers, wells, recharge zones, and surrounding environments. This analysis helps evaluate the sustainability of groundwater use and the possible movement of contaminants through subsurface systems.

Main purpose: to simulate groundwater flow systems, assess well performance, evaluate recharge and discharge patterns, and estimate the potential migration of contaminants in the subsurface.

Key Questions Addressed

  • How does groundwater move within the aquifer system?
  • How will pumping or injection wells influence groundwater levels?
  • Which areas are vulnerable to drawdown or contamination?
  • How far and how fast can contaminants migrate through the subsurface?

Typical Analysis

Groundwater Flow Modelling

Simulation of aquifer behaviour, hydraulic head distribution, recharge, pumping wells, boundary conditions, and groundwater balance.

Contaminant Transport Analysis

Assessment of contaminant movement, plume development, advection, dispersion, and possible risk to wells or sensitive areas.

Why It Is Important

Groundwater is a critical resource for domestic, industrial, agricultural, and environmental needs. Poor understanding of groundwater flow can cause over-extraction, land subsidence, reduced water availability, or conflict between water users.

For contaminated sites, groundwater modelling helps identify the direction and extent of pollutant migration. This supports monitoring plans, remediation strategies, and risk-based decision-making.

Expected Outputs

  • Groundwater level and hydraulic head maps.
  • Flow direction and groundwater balance analysis.
  • Well impact and drawdown assessment.
  • Contaminant plume and migration prediction.
  • Technical recommendations for groundwater management or remediation.

Application Areas

This service can be applied to water supply planning, mining and industrial projects, landfill and waste management sites, groundwater remediation, injection well assessment, and environmental impact studies.
