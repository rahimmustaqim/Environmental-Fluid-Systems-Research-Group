Research-Based Solutions for Water, Coastal, Climate, and Environmental Fluid Systems

What This Company Does

Our business scope covers modelling, analysis, visualization, and technical documentation for environmental and water-related projects.

Coastal and Marine Modelling

Hydrodynamic modelling for tides, waves, tsunami, sediment movement, coastal inundation, and marine hazard assessment.

Hydrology and Flood Analysis

Rainfall-runoff analysis, flood modelling, watershed evaluation, river capacity analysis, and flood mitigation scenarios.

CFD and Environmental Fluid Dynamics

Computational simulation of flow behaviour, turbulence, dispersion, and interaction between fluid motion and engineering structures.

Hydrogeology and Groundwater

Groundwater flow modelling, recharge analysis, well assessment, contaminant transport, and subsurface water system evaluation.

Climate and Environmental Data

Rainfall trend analysis, climate impact interpretation, environmental dataset processing, and support for adaptation studies.

Technical Report and Research Support

Preparation of project reports, scientific figures, manuscripts, publication materials, and research-based technical documentation.
