About Environmental Fluid Systems Research Group

Numerical Modelling, Data Analysis, and Technical Support for Water, Coastal, Hydropower, and Wind Energy Systems

Environmental Fluid Systems Research Group supports engineering consultants, researchers, and public institutions through science-based analysis in hydrology, hydraulic modelling, coastal systems, hydropower, wind energy, and environmental infrastructure.

As a research-based startup, EFSRG is built around focused technical leadership, project-based collaboration, and a flexible capability network for modelling, analysis, training, and scientific reporting.

Leadership Profile

Rahim Mustaqim

CEO & Founder

Rahim Mustaqim leads the technical direction of Environmental Fluid Systems Research Group, with a focus on hydrodynamic analysis, hydraulic modelling, coastal systems, sediment transport, hydrology, and water-related infrastructure assessment.

His work supports research-based engineering studies, technical modelling workflows, training materials, scientific figures, and project documentation for academic, consultant, government, and industry-related collaborations.
